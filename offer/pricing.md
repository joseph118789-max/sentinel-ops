# Sentinel Ops Pricing

## Pricing Strategy
Do not start with custom enterprise pricing chaos.
Start with 3 simple tiers priced in RM/month, anchored around number of monitored servers/endpoints and service depth.

The goal is not perfect pricing. The goal is fast deals, healthy margin, and clean upsell paths.

## Recommended Launch Pricing

### 1. Starter — RM 2,500/month
Best for small businesses with a few critical systems.

Includes:
- up to 5 monitored servers
- managed Wazuh deployment for scoped assets
- basic log monitoring and alert triage
- monthly security summary report
- baseline Linux hardening for in-scope hosts
- email/WhatsApp alert escalation for high-priority issues

Good fit:
- small SMEs
- first-time managed security buyers
- budget-sensitive teams

### 2. Growth — RM 5,000/month
Best default package for early customers.

Includes:
- up to 15 monitored servers
- alert triage and prioritization
- Linux hardening and configuration review
- monthly security and compliance-readiness report
- basic vulnerability visibility
- guided remediation recommendations
- scheduled review call

Good fit:
- companies with production workloads
- teams under customer or audit pressure
- businesses that need more than passive monitoring

### 3. Regulated — RM 8,000 to RM 12,000/month
For customers in regulated or more security-sensitive environments.

Includes:
- up to 25 monitored servers
- stronger reporting and evidence collection
- compliance-readiness mapping support
- prioritized remediation tracking
- tighter escalation workflow
- more hands-on advisory support

Good fit:
- fintech
- healthcare
- logistics with customer audit pressure
- businesses preparing for vendor/security reviews

## One-Time Setup Fee
Recommended: RM 3,000 to RM 8,000 depending on environment complexity.

Why charge setup:
- prevents low-intent prospects
- covers onboarding work
- improves cash flow early
- positions the work as implementation, not "free presales"

If needed, waive part of it only to close a strong pilot.

## 30-Day Pilot Offer
Recommended pilot pricing:
- RM 3,500 for very limited scope
- RM 5,000 for a more realistic pilot

Pilot scope:
- limited number of hosts
- monitoring deployed
- initial hardening review
- findings summary
- action plan

Pilot conversion rule:
- if they continue to monthly service, credit part of pilot fee toward setup or first month

## Commercial Rules
- Minimum term: 3 months after pilot conversion if possible
- Payment upfront monthly
- Scope creep requires repricing
- Keep onboarding scope narrow
- Do not include full incident response retainer in MVP pricing

## Discount Rules
Allowed:
- small pilot credit to reduce friction
- annual prepay discount only if cash matters

Avoid:
- heavy discounts to win logos
- underpricing regulated customers
- unlimited assets/users language

## What Not To Do
Do not:
- price per vague "tenant"
- offer unlimited support in base tier
- include custom SIEM engineering in cheap plans
- promise 24/7 human SOC unless you can actually deliver it

## Default Recommendation
Lead with Growth at RM 5,000/month.
That is usually the cleanest balance of seriousness, value, and margin.
