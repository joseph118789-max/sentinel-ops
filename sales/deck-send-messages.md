# Sentinel Ops — Deck Send Messages

> Two sending modes: (1) initial outreach deck send, (2) post-call deck follow-up.

---

## Mode 1: Deck Send Message
*(WhatsApp or email — sent when sharing the deck for the first time)*

**Long version (email / LinkedIn):**

> Hi [Name],
>

>
> We're focused on Malaysian SMEs and growing teams that have real security pressure but don't need or want the cost and complexity of a full SOC. The pilot is a limited 30-day engagement on a few critical systems — designed to show value fast.
>
> If it feels relevant, happy to jump on a short call.
>
> Best,
> [Joseph's name]

**Short version (WhatsApp):**

> Hi [Name], sharing a short deck on Sentinel Ops — practical monitoring, hardening, and security reporting for lean teams. If it's relevant, happy to walk through a limited 30-day pilot. [attach deck]

## Mode 2: Post-Call Deck Follow-Up
*(Sent immediately after a discovery call — when the prospect wants to review the deck after talking)*

**Subject line (email):**
"Following up — Sentinel Ops overview + next steps"

**Email version:**

> Hi [Name],
>
> Great to speak with you today. As discussed, I'm attaching the Sentinel Ops overview deck.
>
> A few things to keep in mind:
> - The 30-day pilot starts narrow — [3–10] critical Linux servers, agreed scope
> - Pricing is RM 2,000–5,000 depending on scope
> - We handle monitoring, hardening, and a management report
> - No long-term commitment required
>
> The pilot is designed to give you clear visibility and a decision point — continue, expand, or stop.
>
> Let me know if you have any questions after reviewing. Happy to schedule a follow-up call or answer over WhatsApp.
>
> Best,
> [Joseph's name]

**WhatsApp version:**

> Great speaking today! Attaching the deck as discussed. Highlights: 30-day pilot, RM 2,000–5,000, no lock-in. Review it and let me know any questions — we can schedule a follow-up call or I can answer over WhatsApp directly.

## After the Follow-Up — If No Reply (Day 4)

**WhatsApp:**

> Hi [Name], just following up on the deck. No pressure if the timing isn't right — but if security visibility and hardening is something your team is looking at, happy to share more. Either way — good luck with [company's current project/launch/growth].

**Email:**

> Hi [Name], following up on the Sentinel Ops overview. Happy to answer any questions or jump on a short call if it's useful. No pressure at all — just wanted to make sure the information was helpful.

---

## Quick Reference: Which Message to Use When

| Situation | Message |
|-----------|---------|
| First contact + attach deck | Mode 1 — Deck Send |
| Post-discovery call | Mode 2 — Post-Call Follow-Up |
| No reply after 4 days | Day 4 follow-up |
| Prospect says "interested, reviewing internally" | Mode 2 + mention pilot pricing |
| Prospect says "not relevant right now" | Ask why, then close warm |
| Prospect asks for more detail | Send Mode 2 + offer call |
