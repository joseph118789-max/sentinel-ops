## How to Choose Which Message to Send

| Situation | Use |
|-----------|-----|
| Mutual contact introduced you | Message 1 (Warm Intro) |
| CTO/IT Manager, found on LinkedIn | Message 2 (LinkedIn Cold) |
| Phone number only, no prior relationship | Message 3 (WhatsApp Cold) |
| No response after 3 days | Message 4 (Follow-Up) |
| They posted about security/compliance | Message 5 (Post-Event) |

---

## Tips for Better Response Rates

1. **Personalise every message.** Use their name, company, something specific.
2. **Keep it short.** If it looks like a paragraph, cut it in half.
3. **Ask a question.** "Is security on your radar?" performs better than statements.
4. **Don't say "no pressure" too much.** It signals you don't believe in the value.
5. **Send between 9am–11am or 7pm–9pm.** Better response rates than midday.
6. **Follow up once, then stop.** 2 touches is fine. 5 is harassment.
7. **WhatsApp > LinkedIn > Email** (in that order for Malaysian B2B).
